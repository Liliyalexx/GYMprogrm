AI Coaching

Not a chatbot. Four professionals in one.

The AI Coach Alex combines knowledge that typically requires four separate appointments — and applies all of it to your specific body and goals at once.

🏋️
Elite Personal Trainer
Understands the difference between aesthetic shaping and fitness training. Knows which exercises build the look you want — and which ones work against it. Considers exercise order, rep ranges, and rest periods for your specific goal.
🥗
Registered Sports Dietitian
Automatically logs your food when you describe it in chat. Calculates exact macros from your profile using Mifflin-St Jeor with activity and goal adjustments. Knows how caloric deficit affects your training capacity.
🩺
Sports Medicine Doctor
Reads your blood test and doctor prescription. Extracts iron, B12, D3, thyroid markers, and hormonal values. Adjusts training volume and intensity based on what your blood work says about your recovery capacity.
🧬
Functional Medicine + Physio
Understands cortisol response: intense training in a stressed body causes fat retention and muscle breakdown. Identifies postural imbalances and prescribes corrective exercises. Flags contraindicated exercises for your body type and goals.
⚡
How the program generation works
When you ask Alex for a program, it performs an integrated assessment: classifies your goal (aesthetic shaping vs fitness vs fat loss), lists which muscles to GROW and which to CONTROL, evaluates your hormonal risk profile from age + activity + goal, reads your blood test for deficiencies, identifies exercises from the library that would work AGAINST your goal and excludes them — then designs the program. The reasoning behind every decision is shown on your program page.

Getting started

From zero to your personalized program in minutes

No gym membership. No trainer appointment. No app store.

1
Register
Create your account at /members/register/ — name, email, password. That's it. No invite needed.
2
Onboarding
3 steps: your fitness goals → physical stats (age, weight, height) → optionally upload your blood test or doctor prescription.
3
Talk to Alex
Open the AI Chat and describe what you want. Ask for a program, log your meals, ask about your blood test results — Alex handles all of it.
4
Follow your program
Your program appears in the Program tab with animated GIF demonstrations for every exercise. Tick off exercises as you complete them.
5
Track nutrition
Tell Alex what you ate — it logs calories and macros automatically. Your daily targets are calculated from your profile and goal.
6
Posture & progress
Upload a standing photo for AI posture analysis. Track your weekly exercise count and 14-day calorie trend in the Progress tab.
